Our Story

Where It All Began

At Studio Miraycle Art & design; Design, we believe that a space should be felt and live the experience. Our studio creates interiors where art is not decoration, but an essential part of the architecture, shaping identity, atmosphere, and experience through thoughtful emphasis and artistic intention. Every project begins with listening: to the space, to its story, and to the people who will live within it. With a strong emphasis on authenticity, craftsmanship, and hand-crafted details, we design environments that feel personal, timeless, and deeply human. Carefully chosen sustainable materials and intentional focal points create moments of calm and visual emphasis, offering relaxation and a quiet beauty that lingers beyond the first impression. Our work is guided by craftsmanship, respect for tradition, and a deliberate emphasis on integrating art into interior design, creating spaces that age gracefully and leave a meaningful emotional imprint on those who inhabit them.

Meet Our Artist Bio

Dr. Miray Haddad is the Founder and Creative Director of Studio Miraycle Art & Design; a practice dedicated to integrating masterful works of art into interior spaces.

She holds a PhD degree of Doctor of Philosophy in Art and Art Sciences, in addition to degrees in Interior Design and Sacred Art with a focus on conservation and restoration. Her passion for interior design is reflected in her ability to merge creativity, craftsmanship, and precision.

Dr. Haddad is a specialist in traditional and hand-painted stained glass, with expertise in adapting this art form into both modern and classical contexts. She has worked extensively on heritage houses, where her meticulous approach ensures that every project respects and enhances the architectural and cultural identity of the space.

Beyond her studio practice, Dr. Haddad is a professor and coordinator in the Departments of Interior Design and Architecture at the university level. She also serves as a consultant for numerous projects in Lebanon and abroad, ranging from residential and commercial interiors to large-scale art and ornamental painted works.
